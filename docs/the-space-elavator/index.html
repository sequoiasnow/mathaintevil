The Space Elavator

The space elevator is one of the most exiting topics in the future of humanities exploration of space. Not only would it be a feat of engineering that would set a marker for all time of humanities conquest of our solar system, it would make that conquest remarkably cheap and feasible. The general concept of a space elevator is a giant orbiting teether to the earth where spaceships, satellites and astronauts could be lifted by simple electrical power into orbit, and from there be released into space without the need
